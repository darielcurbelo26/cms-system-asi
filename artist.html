THE ARTIST

ISEEASI

ISEEASI is a multimedia artist who builds worlds that encourage self-exploration and connection through shared experience. Moving deliberately away from mass consumption mindsets, she embraces authentic expression developed through thoughtful collaboration with other creatives. Her productions reflect this depth through their scale and immersive nature.

At the heart of her work lies a mission to reveal people to themselves at their own pace. Though her pieces often emerge from periods of isolation, they paradoxically seek to validate the shared nature of personal struggles.
